My Favourite places to see in Egypt

Siwa

In addition to the beauty of the landscape, Siwa is home to attractions of particular historical value, including the fortified city, the Temple of Alexander the Great – where the emperor allegedly consulted the famous oracle – and the Temple of Amun.

Pyramids

Egypt's pharaohs expected to become gods in the afterlife. To prepare for the next world they erected temples to the gods and massive pyramid tombs for themselves—filled with all the things each ruler would need to guide and sustain himself in the next world.

Luxor

Luxor is known as the world's greatest open-air musuem, home to some of Egypt's most famous temples, tombs and monuments.
