Is Jupiter hot or cold?


With an average temperature of minus 234 degrees Fahrenheit

(minus 145 degrees Celsius), Jupiter is frigid even in its warmest

weather. Unlike Earth, whose temperature varies as one moves

closer to or farther from the equator, Jupiter's temperature depends

more on height above the surface
